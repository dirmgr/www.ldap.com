LWN.net has an article about a talk that OpenLDAP chief architect Howard Chu gave at FLOSS 2018 about the upcoming features in OpenLDAP 2.5. Already-committed features include performance improvements and support for RFC 5805 transactions. Additional expected changes include enhancements to the replication engine, new backends, LDAP load-balancing, support for time-based one-time passwords, and certificate generation. Read the article at https://lwn.net/Articles/755207/.
